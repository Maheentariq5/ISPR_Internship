About the Internship

The ISPR Summer Internship 2025 is a 6-week national program organized across 33+ cities, engaging over 6,500 students from 150+ universities. It offers a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in media, communications, journalism, and defense-related public relations.

Duration: July 7 Aug 15, 2025

Timings: Mon/Wed/Fri (10:00 AM 2:00 PM)

Locations: 33+ cities including Islamabad, Lahore, Karachi, Peshawar, Quetta

Fields Eligible: Mass Communication, Media Studies, IR, Journalism, Political Science, Computer Science, Film & TV Production

Why Choose This Internship

The ISPR Internship provides a rare and prestigious opportunity for students to gain hands-on exposure to national-level media and defense communication projects.
Interns are trained in Public Relations, Journalism, and Strategic Communication, while also acquiring practical skills in content creation, video editing, and social media management. The program emphasizes professionalism, discipline, and responsibility, enabling participants to contribute meaningfully to projects of national importance. Additionally, interns will have the chance to network with senior officials and leading media professionals, creating pathways for future careers in defense communication, media, and public relations. This internship not only enhances academic learning but also helps students develop a professional portfolio that reflects both competence and commitment to excellence.
