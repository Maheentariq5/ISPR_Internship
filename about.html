Lieutenant General

Ahmed Sharif Chaudhry

Director General ISPR


Ahmed Shareef Chaudhry HI(M) is a three-star General in the Pakistan Army. He is currently serving as the 22nd Director General of ISPR since 6 December 2022. He belongs to Pakistan Army Corps of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering. In 2022, Chaudhry became the first one from the EME corps to be appointed as the DG ISPR.
